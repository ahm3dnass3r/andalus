What we do

services

At Al-Andalus, we specialize in providing comprehensive solutions in sea shipping, logistics, stevedoring, and Suez Canal transit. Our experienced team ensures efficient cargo handling, smooth passage through strategic waterways, and reliable global shipping operations. With a commitment to excellence and precision, Al-Andalus is your trusted partner in navigating the complexities of the maritime industry

Shipping & Logistics

At Al-Andalus, we offer seamless sea shipping and logistics solutions designed to move your goods efficiently across the globe. From secure, timely ocean freight to end-to-end logistics management, we optimize your supply chain, ensuring smooth transportation, warehousing, and distribution. With a focus on safety, cost-effectiveness, and reliability, Al-Andalus delivers excellence at every step of your cargo’s journey.

Stevedoring

Al-Andalus offers expert stevedoring services, ensuring the efficient loading and unloading of cargo at ports. Our skilled team manages the safe handling and transfer of goods, optimizing turnaround times and minimizing delays. With our attention to detail and commitment to safety, we ensure your cargo is in capable hands from ship to shore

suez canal transit

Al-Andalus specializes in facilitating smooth and efficient Suez Canal transit for vessels. Our team navigates the complexities of this crucial waterway, ensuring timely passage and minimizing delays. With in-depth knowledge of canal regulations and operations, we provide seamless support for your ship’s journey through one of the world's most strategic maritime routes.
